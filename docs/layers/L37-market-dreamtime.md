# Layer 37 — Market Dreamtime

**Core innovation:** Collective altered-state weekend exploration.

On weekends (closed markets), the ecosystem enters Dreamtime: accelerated replay of the week's events at 100×–1000× real-time, with optional alien-rule perturbations. Agents dream their week and produce compressed reflections and new hypotheses.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Dreamtime sessions are deterministic replays + documented perturbations.
- Each Dreamtime session produces at least one new hypothesis for Prometheus to evaluate on Monday.
- Dreamtime UI overlay (purple-cyan aurora) is only visible when active.
