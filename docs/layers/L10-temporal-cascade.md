# Layer 10 — Temporal Cascade

**Core innovation:** Branching parallel realities.

Whenever an important decision is made (a strategy promotion, a regime classification, a Hidden-Hand injection), the ecosystem forks a short-lived "what-if" branch where the opposite decision is taken. The branch runs in a sandbox on a time horizon of minutes to hours; its outcome is recorded.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every Tier-Medium+ decision spawns at least one cascade branch.
- Cascade branches are deterministic and replayable.
- Branches are pruned after 24 h unless promoted to permanent Principles.
