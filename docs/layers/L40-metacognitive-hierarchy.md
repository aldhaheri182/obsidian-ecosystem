# Layer 40 — Metacognitive Hierarchy

**Core innovation:** Recursive thinking about thinking.

Each city has a dedicated "metacognition" agent that does not trade or research directly; its sole job is to monitor its own city's reasoning processes for biases, blind spots, and systematic errors. Metacognition agents publish critiques that the city CEO can act on.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every city has a registered metacognition agent by M7.
- Metacognition reports are published weekly as Principles with `type=METACOGNITION`.
- A city ignoring a metacognition critique for 4 consecutive weeks triggers a Global CEO escalation.
