# Layer 41 — Dream of the System

**Core innovation:** Annual collective ecosystem dream.

Once per year, on the Civilization Epoch rollover (L33), the ecosystem enters a week-long collective Dreamtime where every agent dreams simultaneously. The dreams are stitched together by Mnemosyne's L3 Summarizer into a single annual narrative.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Annual dream produces one canonical Epoch Principle — the civilization's self-understanding for the coming year.
- Every agent contributes at least one dream fragment.
- The Epoch Principle is broadcast as an Advancement Orb to every city.
