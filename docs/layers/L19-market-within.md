# Layer 19 — The Market Within

**Core innovation:** Internal micro-economy with token trading.

An internal prediction-market where agents can stake AIX (L23) on propositions (e.g., "momentum will outperform mean-reversion in the next 30 days"). Prices reveal the ecosystem's collective confidence on any claim.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Fully matched, signed, and logged on an internal exchange agent.
- No external settlement; AIX-only.
- Owner may always liquidate positions in an emergency.
