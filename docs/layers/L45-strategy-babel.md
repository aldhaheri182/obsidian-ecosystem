# Layer 45 — Strategy Babel

**Core innovation:** Exhaustive combinatorial strategy catalog.

A catalog that enumerates strategies across a defined combinatorial space: (asset class × signal family × regime × horizon × execution style). Prometheus systematically instantiates entries that have not yet been tested, runs CPCV, and archives results — whether positive, negative, or null.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Catalog space is documented in-repo and versioned.
- Negative results are preserved as first-class Principles.
- Random sampling across the catalog guarantees eventual coverage.
