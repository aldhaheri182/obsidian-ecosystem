# Layer 13 — Legacy Forge

**Core innovation:** Owner continuity and digital twin.

A curated long-form corpus of the Owner's decisions, rationales, and strategic preferences — accumulated across years — used to (a) preserve continuity if the Owner becomes unavailable, and (b) train a digital twin that advisory agents can consult (read-only).

## Acceptance Criteria

- Corpus storage is encrypted at rest, requires Owner key to access.
- Twin is strictly advisory; it cannot issue executive directives.
- Yearly export to cold storage outside the ecosystem.
