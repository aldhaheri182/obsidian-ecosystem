# Layer 26 — Chimera Forge

**Core innovation:** Synthetic market generation for radical robustness.

## Scope

A generative system that produces synthetic market data — statistically plausible price series that never occurred in history, generated by GANs and diffusion models — and "alien markets" governed by arbitrary mathematical rules (fluid dynamics, biological evolution, discrete chaos) to force agents to develop radical robustness.

## Generators

- **GAN-based** — trained on decades of real price series; produces novel but statistically compatible universes.
- **Diffusion-based** — conditional generation over regime labels (LOW_VOL_BULL, CRISIS, etc.).
- **Alien-rule generators** — synthetic universes where price obeys e.g. Navier-Stokes turbulence or Lotka-Volterra predator-prey dynamics.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Synthetic fidelity: match real stylized facts (heavy tails, volatility clustering, leverage effect) within ±5%.
- Agents that overfit to real data must degrade on synthetic data — this is the stress test.
- A strategy must maintain ≥60% of its real-data Sharpe on at least 3 independent alien-rule universes before promotion.
