## 11.2 The Six View Layers

| Layer | Scale | Rendering Technology | Description |
|:------|:------|:---------------------|:------------|
| **Orbit View** | Planet | PixiJS 2D | The entire planet with 11 city nodes, P&L aurora, advancement orbs, Civilization Timeline ring |
| **Nexus Overworld** | Continent | PixiJS 2D | 2D map with city icons, roads, message packet animations, city health auras |
| **City View** | City | Three.js 3D Isometric | Isometric chamber grid, 3D buildings with emissive windows, agent sprite capsules, packet spheres with trails |
| **Building View** | Building | Three.js 3D | Cutaway interior, workstations with monitors and whiteboards, meeting rooms, server racks |

## 11.4 Motion Language

- **Agent movement**: Bespoke cubic bezier `(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1.0)` — deliberate, weighted deceleration.
- **Message packets**: `(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1.0)` — rapid acceleration, smooth deceleration.
- **UI panels**: `(0.33, 0.0, 0.67, 1.0)` — smooth, no bounce.
- **Alerts and overrides**: Spring physics — mass 1, stiffness 300, damping 20. Hard impact, fast settle.
- **Building breath**: Subtle 0.5% scale oscillation on a 4-second sine cycle.
- **Status auras**: Continuous 1.5-second alpha pulse (0.15–0.5).

## 11.5 Audio Design

- **Ambient drones**: Low C (65.41 Hz) in Orbit View with dual detuned oscillators and slow LFO modulation. G2 (98 Hz) triangle wave in City View.
- **Message whoosh**: 50ms white noise burst, bandpass filtered at 2kHz, priority-based playback rate.
- **Advancement chime**: Ascending three-note C-E-G sine arpeggio, 120ms per note.
- **Risk alarm**: 80Hz bass hit with simultaneous 1200Hz ping, repeated every 500ms during override.
- **Agent birth/retirement**: Soft high chime for birth; slow low bell for retirement.
- **Market open/close**: Crisp 880Hz bell for open; soft 587Hz bell for close.
- **Emergency Stop**: Total silence for 2 seconds, then deep sustained 55Hz tone.

## 11.6 Day/Night Cycle

Lighting parameters evolve continuously through a 24-hour cycle:

- **Market hours (09:30–16:00 ET)**: Full brightness, ambient 0.4, sun intensity 1.5, ghost white sun color.
- **Dusk (16:00–17:30)**: Gradual 90-minute transition. Ambient drops to 0.15. Sun color shifts through ember. Street lamps fade in.
- **Night (17:30–05:00)**: Darkest. Ambient 0.12. Sun intensity 0.2. Sun color shifts to deep amethyst. Building windows and street lamps provide primary illumination.
- **Dawn (05:00–09:30)**: Gradual 270-minute transition back to full brightness.
- **Dreamtime (Weekends)**: Purple-cyan aurora overlay. All buildings tinted amethyst.

## 11.7 Weather Effects

- **Dust motes**: Always present. 200 particles per district. 1-2px, lunar silver at 15% opacity. Slow random drift upward.
- **Rain during drawdowns**: Intensity proportional to daily P&L loss. 50 particles (light) to 500 particles (heavy). 8-16px vertical strokes in grey-blue. Fades in/out over 30-60 seconds.
- **Fireflies (Agent Graveyard, night only)**: 20-30 golden particles with random walk and subtle twinkle. 2px core with 6px glow.
