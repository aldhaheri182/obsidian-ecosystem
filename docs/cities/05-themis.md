# City 5: Themis — Governance & Compliance

Themis ensures the ecosystem operates within legal, regulatory, and ethical boundaries. It is the conscience and the compliance officer of the civilization.

## Agents

- `rule-enforcer` (Python): Maintains knowledge base of trading rules per jurisdiction. Checks every order before execution.
- `legal-auditor` (Python): Generates periodic regulatory reports (MiFID II, SEC Rule 606, etc.).
- `ethics-watchdog` (Python): Evaluates strategies against owner-defined ethical constraints.
- `regulatory-monitor` (Python): Scrapes regulatory websites for new rules, updates rule engine.
- `filing-agent` (Python): Auto-generates and submits required regulatory filings.
- `whistleblower-receptionist` (Python): Accepts anonymous internal reports of system misbehavior.

## Philosophical Engine (Layer 25)

Before any strategy is deployed with real capital, the Philosophical Engine evaluates it against multiple ethical frameworks:

- **Utilitarianism**: Does the strategy maximize overall welfare?
- **Deontology (Kantian)**: Does the strategy respect the autonomy of other market participants?
- **Virtue Ethics**: Does the strategy embody virtues like prudence, justice, and temperance?
- **Game Theory**: Does the strategy rely on manipulation or deception?

A strategy that passes statistical validation but fails ethical review is flagged for Owner attention.
