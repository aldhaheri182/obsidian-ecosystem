# City 3: Prometheus — Research & Innovation

Prometheus is the discovery engine. It automates the entire research pipeline: hypothesis generation, backtesting, dream simulation, adversarial testing, and paper replication. It is the reason the system can evolve beyond its initial seed strategies.

## Districts

- **Hypothesis Lab.** Where the Hypothesis Generator agent formulates new strategy ideas. LLM-powered, but outputs structured strategy templates, not freeform code.
- **Dream Simulator.** A sandbox environment that replays historical market data at accelerated speed (up to 1000× real-time). Agents "dream" by re-living historical scenarios and testing their strategies against them.
- **Backtest Arena.** Massively parallel backtesting infrastructure. Every strategy undergoes Combinatorially Purged Cross-Validation (CPCV) with embargo periods, Deflated Sharpe Ratio (DSR) computation, and Probability of Backtest Overfitting (PBO) assessment.
- **Adversarial Colosseum.** Where new strategies face adversarial testing agents that actively try to find edge cases, fragility points, and overfitting.
- **Paper Replication Office.** Reads academic quantitative finance papers, extracts strategy logic, and replicates both the strategy and its validation protocol on the ecosystem's data universe.
- **Chimera Forge.** Generates synthetic market data — statistically plausible price series that never occurred in history, generated by GANs and diffusion models. Also generates "alien markets" governed by arbitrary mathematical rules (fluid dynamics, biological evolution) to force agents to develop radical robustness.

## Agent Inventory

- `hypothesis-generator` (Python, LLM-powered): Formulates testable strategy hypotheses based on knowledge gaps, recent performance degradation, and cross-domain inspiration.
- `backtest-runner` (Python, parallelized with Ray/Dask): Runs strategies against historical data with strict CPCV protocols.
- `dream-explorer` (Python): Manages Dream Room sessions for agents requesting historical replay.
- `paper-replicator` (Python, LLM-powered): Extracts strategies from academic papers, validates on current data.
- `adversarial-tester` (Python): Finds weaknesses in proposed strategies.
- `overfitting-detector` (Python): Computes DSR, PBO, family-wise error rates.
- `feature-ideation-agent` (Python): Watches ecosystem and suggests new features to the Owner.

## C-Suite

- `ceo-prometheus` (Python, LLM-powered)
- `cso-prometheus`: Evaluates research quality, prioritizes exploration.
- `cto-prometheus`: Manages simulation infrastructure.

## Visual Metaphor

A futuristic research campus with glass domes (Dream Simulators), lightning strikes within the domes (breakthroughs flashing), a Colosseum for adversarial testing, and the Chimera Forge glowing with synthetic data generation.
