# City 10: Eris — Inverse Adversary

Eris is the adversary. It is a sealed, firewalled city where every agent is the inverse of its Aletheia counterpart. Its purpose is not to make money — it is to break things, find weaknesses, and force the main ecosystem to be robust.

## Agents (all inverse of Aletheia)

- `inverse-momentum-signal`: Outputs the opposite direction.
- `inverse-risk-overlord`: Encourages risk-taking rather than constraining it.
- `inverse-portfolio-optimizer`: Minimizes Sharpe, maximizes drawdown.

All Aletheia Signal Agents and Guardian Agents have inverse counterparts in Eris, deployed in an isolated namespace with no access to real capital.

## War Games

Quarterly, Eris and Aletheia compete for a simulated capital pool. If Eris wins, its inverse logic is studied for valuable inversions. Eris never trades real capital.

## Visual Metaphor

A dark, inverted spire pointing downward beneath the planet surface. Crimson energy pulses upward. Firewalled from all other cities with visible security barriers.
