# City 11: Janus — Inter-Reality Bridge

Janus connects the digital ecosystem to the physical world. It ingests data from IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and human experts. In its most advanced form (tightly sandboxed), it can control physical assets.

## Agents

- `iot-ingestor` (Python): Ingests sensor data from factories, shipping GPS, energy grids.
- `satellite-imagery-processor` (Python, CV models): Analyzes satellite images for retail parking lot fullness, crop health, oil tanker counts.
- `human-expert-interface` (Python): API for querying domain experts via paid micro-consultations.
- `physical-actuator-controller` (Python, sandboxed): Controls physical assets (drones, energy dispatch). Requires multi-signature authorization: board consensus + Owner acknowledgment + hardware security key.

## Visual Metaphor

A two-faced gateway at the edge of the planet. One face looks inward at the digital cities. The other looks outward at the physical world. Data streams flow in both directions.

## Command Center Chamber (MV)

- Accent color: Split — left side Silver, right side Deep Blue `#0B1D3A`.
- Interior: Two-faced archway. One side: miniature satellite + sensor array. Other side: small factory arm (simple robotic arm).
- Agents (2): One silver suit, one deep-blue suit, on opposite sides of the arch.
- Animations: Robotic arm moves a tiny cube back and forth; sensor array rotates.
- Props: archway, satellite, robotic arm.
